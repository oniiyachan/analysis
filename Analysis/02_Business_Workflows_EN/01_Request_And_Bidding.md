# BUSINESS WORKFLOW: REQUEST & LIVE BIDDING

> **Overview**: The primary conversion funnel translating user cleaning intents into competitive subcontractor clashes.

## 1. THE WIZARD (Quote Setup)
An 8-step frictional-less form collecting dimensions, services, and locations. Maintains state in memory before executing a unified DB `INSERT`.

## 2. THE ARENA (Live Bidding)
Partners within geospatial boundaries receive silent push notifications. They submit competitive `quotes` causing the Customer's waiting room UI to live-update via WebSockets.

## 3. THE SHOWDOWN (Comparison)
Users compare up to 5 finalized bids side-by-side evaluating pricing against Partner Ratings and Review aggregates.
