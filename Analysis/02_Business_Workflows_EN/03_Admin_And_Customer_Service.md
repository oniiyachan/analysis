# BUSINESS WORKFLOW: ADMIN & DISPUTE RESOLUTION

> **Overview**: Internal corporate governance and damage control.

## 1. KANBAN DISPATCH
Admins monitor un-matched orders. They reserve the authority to forcefully assign high-priority "orphan" cleanings directly to premium Partners.

## 2. DISPUTE COURT
Users flagging sub-par cleanings trigger CS tickets. Admins hold powers to issue partial or complete NicePay `Refunds`, subsequently debiting the Partner's future settlement payloads.

## 3. PENAL MATRIX
Partners generating excessive refunds or abusive chat behavior face forced Account Suspensions via the `Members` dashboard modifying Supabase Auth states directly.
